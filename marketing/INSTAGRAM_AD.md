## Visual Content Guidelines

### Image Specifications
- **Format**: Square (1:1) or Vertical (4:5)
- **Resolution**: 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1350px (vertical)
- **Style**: Clean, modern, professional
- **Colors**: Use the section colors (blue, green, purple, red, yellow, etc.)

### Content Ideas
1. **Screenshot Collage**: Show all 10 sections in a grid
2. **Animated Story**: Swipe through each section
3. **Before/After**: Scattered bookmarks vs. organized portal
4. **Video**: 15-30 second screen recording showing the site

### Caption Best Practices
- Keep it concise (Instagram limits to 2,200 characters)
- Use emojis strategically
- Include clear call-to-action
- Add relevant hashtags (5-10 recommended)
