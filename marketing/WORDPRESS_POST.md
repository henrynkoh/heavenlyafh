### Introduction

Managing an Adult Family Home (AFH) operation requires access to numerous resources, regulations, and links scattered across different websites and platforms. From WAC 388-76 compliance information to DSHS forms, from REC training programs to ALTSA resources - keeping track of everything can be overwhelming.

**Heavenly AFH** solves this problem by providing a single, organized landing page where all your essential resources are just one click away.

### The Challenge

As an AFH provider, administrator, or staff member, you've likely experienced:

- **Bookmark Overload**: Important links saved across multiple browsers and devices
- **Time Waste**: Spending valuable minutes searching for the right form or regulation
- **Missed Deadlines**: Forgetting where important resources are located
- **Compliance Risks**: Difficulty staying up-to-date with changing regulations

These challenges not only impact productivity but can also affect the quality of care you provide.

### The Solution: Heavenly AFH

Heavenly AFH is a modern, responsive web application designed specifically for the AFH community in Washington State. It consolidates all your essential resources into **10 organized, color-coded sections**:

#### 1. AFH Section (Blue)
Adult Family Home specific resources, regulations, and guidelines

#### 2. WAC 388-76 Section (Green)
Complete information about WAC 388-76 regulations and compliance requirements

#### 3. REC Section (Purple)
Regional Education Center programs, training opportunities, and resources

#### 4. IBC Section (Red)
International Building Code standards and compliance information

#### 5. RCS Section (Yellow)
Residential Care Services information, regulations, and forms

#### 6. ALTSA Section (Indigo)
Aging and Long-Term Support Administration resources and programs

#### 7. DSHS Section (Teal)
Department of Social and Health Services links, forms, and information

#### 8. WABO Section (Orange)
Washington Association of Building Officials standards and resources

#### 9. Resources Section (Pink)
General resources, training materials, and documentation

#### 10. Quick Links Section (Cyan)
Emergency contacts, important updates, and frequently accessed links
