## Newsletter Best Practices

### Timing
- **Best Days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best Times**: 9-10 AM or 2-3 PM
- **Frequency**: Monthly or bi-weekly

### Content Tips
- Keep it concise (people scan, not read)
- Use bullet points for easy scanning
- Include clear call-to-action buttons
- Add visual elements (screenshots, graphics)
- Personalize when possible

### Engagement
- Ask questions to encourage replies
- Include social sharing buttons
- Provide multiple ways to contact
- Track open rates and clicks

---

## A/B Testing Ideas

1. **Subject Lines**: Test different subject line styles
2. **Content Length**: Short vs. detailed versions
3. **CTA Placement**: Top vs. bottom of email
4. **Visual Elements**: With images vs. text-only
